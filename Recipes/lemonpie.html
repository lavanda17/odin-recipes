Lemon Pie

Lemon pie, perfect and easy recipe for summer, a not so healthy must-have for every recipe book.

1:30hs

Ingredients

For the dough

  • 300g of all purpose flour
  • 200g of butter
  • teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt
  • 200g of sugar
  • 1 yolk

For the filling

  • 500ml of milk
  • 200ml of lemon juice
  • 200g of sugar
  • 60g of cornstarch
  • 1 lemon
  • 4 yolks

For decoration

  • 4 egg whites
  • 240g of sugar

Making

  1. Dough. Mix sugar with oily butter, then add the vanilla extract, salt and yolk. Mix again, then add the flour. Integrate without kneading much and put in refrigerator. Stretch and put on a mould. Slightly puncture with a fork, and cook for fifteen minutes on oven between 150-180Âºc, with wieght over dough.

  2. Filling. Over heat, mix milk and half of the sugar. Aside, beat the yolks with the other half of the sugar, the lemon zest and juice and cornstarch. Add milk and sugar to the other mix, and integrate. Put everything back on the pot and cook, while stirring, until it starts thickening, then let it boil for a couple of minutes. Cover and put on refigerator.

  3. Decoration. Mix sugar and egg whites, do not whip. Put on microwaave for 30, then mix again. Repeat 4 times more. Then whip until mounted.

  4. Finishing. Fill the dough with filling, add meringue and decorate. Use cooking blowtorch, if available.
